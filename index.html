About


Thresholds Design is focused on designing products & software for the intersection of not only the digital and the physical but of art and science. We strive to create meaningful work, using current and frontier technologies, that improves everyday life.


Henry Boyd is a designer/developer, writer, and general tinkerer currently based in Colorado.
