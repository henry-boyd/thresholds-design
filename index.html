About


Thresholds Design is about designing products & software at the intersection of the digital and the physical as well as art and science. We strive to create meaningful work, using current and frontier technologies, that improves everyday life.


Henry Boyd is a designer/developer, writer, and gadget tinkerer currently based in Colorado (though he is known to wander).
